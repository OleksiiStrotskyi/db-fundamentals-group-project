Notes

Training

Room is optional
⸻
Trainer → Training Schedule
1 : N
One trainer can conduct many scheduled training sessions. Each scheduled training session is conducted by one trainer.
Training → Training Schedule
1 : N
One training type (group class or personal training) can have many scheduled sessions. Each schedule entry belongs to one training type.
Trainer ↔ Specialization
M : N
Trainers may have multiple specializations, and one specialization may belong to multiple trainers.
Room → Training Schedule (optional)
1 : N
One room can host many scheduled training sessions. Each scheduled session takes place in one room.
